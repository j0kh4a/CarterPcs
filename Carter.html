About Carter

The guy behind the builds. Tech content, daily uploads, and setups that actually go hard.

Carter Ryan Smith (born August 8, 2005), known online as CarterPCs, is an American tech creator from California.

He started posting PC and tech content on TikTok in 2020 and grew to millions of followers by uploading four videos every single day — no days off. Ever.

Carter is known for tech reviews, setup tours, and making even boring tech topics genuinely entertaining. In 2024 he won a sub race against Nathan Espinoza, hitting 1 million YouTube subscribers first.
